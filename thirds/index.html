United States of America

Divided in thirds by density


One third of the U.S. population lives in high density block groups, or 0.34% of the total land area of the United States.
One third of the U.S. population lives in medium density block groups, or 1.32% of the total land area of the United States.
One third of the U.S. population lives in low density block groups, or 98.34% of the total land area of the United States.
Using the 2015 American Community Survey data, all U.S. Census block groups were sorted by population density and then divided in thirds with each third having approximately equal population of about 105 million.
